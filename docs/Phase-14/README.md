## 2. Core Functional Additions in Phase 14

* **SaaS Pricing Experience**: Highly-tailored Student (Free to Premium), Professional Tutor (Starter to Elite), and Institutional (Business to Enterprise) billing structures with monthly and 20% discounted annual toggles.
* **Unified "My Plan" Hub**: Dashboard replacing user subscriptions, consolidating usage meters (worksheets uploaded, AI requests, tutor hours), billing history with text/print receipts, and payment card simulators.
* **Socratic AI Homework Workspace**: Expanded flagship assistant with multi-format media uploads supporting images, PDFs, math sheets, and handwritten assignments, complete with a glassmorphic upgrade block for non-subscribed students.
* **Intelligent Study Vault**: Subject-categorized (Math, Science, English, etc.) archiver displaying detailed mastery progress indicators, chronological filters, text search, and details drawers to reopen historic AI walkthrough responses.
* **Administrative Telemetry Cockpit**: Analytical dashboard subtab displaying Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR), Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), plan-based income distribution, subscriber churn ratios, and live subject popularity counters.
