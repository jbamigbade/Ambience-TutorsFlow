# Character Education AI

Ambience TutorsFlow™ includes character education as part of whole-child development.

## Focus Areas

- Integrity
- Responsibility
- Kindness
- Perseverance
- Leadership
- Respect
- Discipline
- Service

## AI Support

The AI can generate reflection prompts, parent encouragement notes, tutor observations, scripture-based reflections, and character growth summaries.

## Purpose

To support academic excellence while also encouraging strong values and godly character.
